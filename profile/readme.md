# Polymarket Community

Welcome to **Polymarket Community**.

This organization was created for people who believe prediction markets are more than just trading — they are **a new way to understand the world**.

Polymarket is where information, probability, and human belief meet.  
Behind every market there are **ideas, research, intuition, and strategy**.

This community exists so **developers and traders can build, learn, and grow together**.

---

## Why this community exists

Many people trade.  
Many people build tools.  

But few places exist where **builders and traders meet in one open community**.

Here we want to create a space where people can:

- Share **trading strategies**
- Build **analytics tools**
- Create **bots and integrations**
- Explore **market data**
- Discuss **ideas and probabilities**
- Learn from **wins and losses**

Whether you are a **developer**, a **quant**, or a **curious trader**, you belong here.

---

## What you will find here

Inside this organization you may find projects like:

- Polymarket data tools
- Trading bots
- SDKs and APIs
- Research and strategy discussions
- Market analytics dashboards
- Automation tools

Everyone is welcome to contribute, experiment, and share knowledge.

---

## Join the community

Anyone can join this organization.

Simply open a join request here:

https://github.com/Polymarket-Community/Join-Community

Then:

1. Click **New Issue**
2. Select **"Join Organization"**
3. Submit the issue

Once approved, you will receive an **organization invitation**.

We believe **communities grow through openness**, so joining is intentionally simple.
